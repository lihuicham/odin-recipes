Make Bacon Pancakes for Breakfast !

Description

These savory bacon pancakes are quick and easy to make.

It's my favorite buttermilk pancakes recipe with crispy bacon crumbs added.

You only need 10 ingredients !

Ingredients

  • 10 slices bacon
  • 2 cups buttermilk, at room temperature
  • 3 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°F (100°C). Line a baking sheet with foil and place inside the oven.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, eggs, maple syrup, and vanilla until combined. Set aside.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and grease with butter.
  4. Pipe whipped cream on top if desired.
  5. Serve immediately or store covered at room temperature for up to 1 day.
