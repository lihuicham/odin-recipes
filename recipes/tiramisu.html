Make A Tiramisu Cake !

Description

This Tiramisu Cake is made of 5 ingredient genoise cake layers brushed with espresso and filled with irresistibly creamy coffee mascarpone cream.

No raw eggs in the frosting. Just 10 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 cups full-fat mascarpone

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 350°F / 175°C.
  • Make the sponge fingers.
  • Make the mascarpone frosting
  • Pipe whipped cream on top if desired.
  • Chill and serve !
